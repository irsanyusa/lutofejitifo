20 People Who Proudly Followed in Their Parents Footsteps, and Its More Than Moving

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Some children look like mini versions of their parents when they’re small but some kids go further and follow in their parents’ footsteps as adults. They become police officers, pilots, teachers, and DJs to be more like their role models and make their parents proud. Some even continue a long-standing family tradition of passing the same job down from generation to generation!

We at Bright Side are fascinated by touching family stories and we’d like to share some of them to prove that some things just run in the family.

9. “My sister teaches at the same high school I went to. Here she is, following in our parents’ protesting footsteps.”

10. “My father was in law enforcement for 40+ years. My sister just graduated from the academy! So proud of her!”

11. “3 years ago I made a post about my father and how he was my hero. 3 years later I’m following in his footsteps and working with him.”
