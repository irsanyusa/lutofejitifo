Jules Sylvester (born 13 November 1950) is a snake wrangler who works in film and television.

He has appeared in numerous television shows including a series of appearances on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno and his own 2002 series Wild Adventures. 
He handled the snakes in the film Snakes on a Plane.
