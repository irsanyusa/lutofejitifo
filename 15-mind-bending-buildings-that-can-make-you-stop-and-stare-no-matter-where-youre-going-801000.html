15+ Mind-Bending Buildings That Can Make You Stop and Stare, No Matter Where Youre Going

Publish date: 2024-06-30

As time goes by, designers and architects are getting more and more creative and bold with experiments. Gurus of modern architecture can give us more than just beautiful, functional houses. Buildings nowadays can make faces, melt, dance, and play music — and the list of their “talents” seems to have no end.

We at Bright Side would definitely not pass by these stunning buildings without stopping to stare at them for at least for a couple of minutes, and we wonder if they fascinate you too. Be sure not to miss our bonus section where we’ll share the secret of the “sinking” house in Paris, France.

1. This house in Paris, France looks like it’s melting. It’s actually a monument.

2. This hotel in Amsterdam, which is in the Netherlands, looks like dozens of little cottages that were merged together.

3. An architectural monument in Portugal that uses 2 giant boulders as its walls and ceiling

4. This upside-down house in Brighton, in the UK, is a museum of illusions.

5. This building in Prague, in the Czech Republic, is called the Dancing House.

6. This wavy building is part of a shopping center in Sopot, Poland.

10. It looks like this building in Tel Aviv, Israel combines all geometric shapes.

11. This skyscraper in New York (in the US) looks like a big Jenga game.

12. A building in Batumi, Georgia that looks like it has a face

13. This landmark building in Birmingham, in the UK, is part of a shopping center, and it looks like it’s covered in bubble wrap.

14. This building seems to be flat because of the photo angle.

In fact, it has a triangle shape.

15. This house in Dresden, Germany “plays music” when it rains thanks to its unusual drain system.

16. The Athenaeum Hotel in London, in the UK, is partially covered in plants.
