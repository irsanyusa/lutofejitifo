Apart from stand-up routines, the comedy in Got Talent competitions also rests in unconventional, yet entertaining acts each season. It is for this reason that naked men and their funny dances have always been a showstopper.

We compiled the most engaging towel dances of nude men in Got Talent franchises all over the world. Let’s take a look at them.

Top Naked Men’s Towel Dances on ‘Got Talent’

1. Les Beaux Freres, America’s Got Talent 2021

Although they were eliminated during the deliberations, Les Beaux Feres weren’t easily forgotten. This duo captivated both the judges and audience in their audition when they strategically covered their genitals with towels. At the end of their act, one of them was left onstage completely undressed, struggling to get a towel to cover himself up.

2. Mama’s Boyz, Pilipinas Got Talent 2018

Back in Pilipinas Got Talent season 6, Mama’s Boyz managed to tickle the funny bone of the crowd with their comedic towel dance. Impressed by the bold act, this group advanced in the next round of the competition.

3. Men with Pans, America’s Got Talent 2017

Kevin Brooking and Colm O’Grady were the funny men wearing chef hats during season 12 of America’s Got Talent. Calling their act as “Naked Lunch,” these two have creatively pulled their novelty performance. Mel B and Simon Cowell buzzed the act, but they nevertheless gave the audience a great show.

4. Stripboys, Asia’s Got Talent 2017

In an attempt to prove that dancing while naked is real talent, Stripboys joined Asia’s Got Talent season 2. This group of cheeky dancers has effectively made their towel dance performance a “clean fun” one. Judge David Foster, who wasn’t even a fan of male-stripping, said yes to their act.

5. Les Beaux Freres, France’s Got Talent 2014

Before gracing AGT 2021, Les Beaux Freres first appeared in France’s Got Talent. Like how they fascinated the crowd in AGT, the duo has successfully made people laugh with their performance.

6. Daniel and Donald, Romania’s Got Talent 2018

Daniel and Donald are a duo of nude men who bravely took their talent to stage in Romania’s Got Talent. With their funny and compelling towel dance moves, they indeed managed to keep the audience entertained until the end of their performance.

7. Artem Strogin and Vladimir Stuka, Ukraine’s Got Talent 2015

Artem Strogin and Vladimir Stuka are practically the Ukraine version of other naked men doing towel dances. Wearing just red bow ties and socks, this duo made the audience nervous and lively at the same time with their act.
