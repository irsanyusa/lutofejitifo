Call for Entries - Nigeria International Gospel Music Awards 2023

Publish date: 2024-08-27

This announcement was officially conveyed today by Mr Victor Igbinigie (GospoGroove) who serves as the project director of this prestigious award. Participation in this award is open to all Gospel Music artists based in Nigeria and those of Nigerian descent residing abroad.

The Nigeria International Gospel Music Awards (NIGMA) proudly upholds its distinguished reputation as the paramount and most revered awards ceremony within the Nigerian Gospel Music industry.

The esteemed Academy of Gospel Music in Nigeria is the driving force behind The Nigeria International Gospel Music Awards (NIGMA). Pulse Nigeria

ADVERTISEMENT

NIGMA remains devoted to honouring the global impact of Nigerian gospel music, consistently identifying and applauding the extraordinary accomplishments of Nigerian gospel artists who have achieved widespread recognition and positively impacted lives well beyond our nation's borders.

The awards ceremony is scheduled to take place at the Shell Hall of the Muson Centre in Onikan, Lagos, on November 19, 2023. This revamped awards ceremony will continue the tradition of celebrating outstanding achievements in the gospel music sector.

Participation in this award is open to all Gospel Music artists based in Nigeria and those of Nigerian descent residing abroad. Pulse Nigeria

Year in review: Between January 2022 and August 2023. For more information and the submission process, kindly visit www.nigmawards.com.
