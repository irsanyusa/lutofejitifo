Comedian AY's Wife, Mabel Makun, Hints on Why She Left Her Matrimonial Home: &quot;Marriage Is Hard&quot;

Publish date: 2024-09-02
  • Mabel Makun, the wife of Nigerian comedian Ayo Makun, has shared why she left her home
  • In a video on her Instagram page, she posted a clip about distance being her response to disrespect
  • Mabel also noted that she does not react or dive into drama, as she simply removes herself from the situation

Mabel Makun, an interior decor expert and the wife of a popular Nigerian comedian Ayo Makun, aka AY, has responded to the rumours of her marriage breakup.

The mother of two posted a video on her Instagram page noting that distance was her response to disrespect.

She stated that she does not involve herself in drama, nor does she respond to it. Instead, she takes herself off the situation.

Her statement came about an hour after her husband confirmed the rumours of their marriage hitting the rocks.
