The Masked Singer has shared a sneak peek from this week’s Season 11 premiere, featuring a performance of Olivia Rodrigo’s song “Vampire” by Goldfish. The panelists were clearly blown away by their voice.

Goldfish Performs in The Masked Singer Sneak Peek

The Goldfish costume features a fish head with large ears, a pink mohawk, and dramatic eyelashes, paired with a ruffled pink dress made to look like fish scales and fins. As they performed, the stage effects were reminiscent of water.

Goldfish showed off an incredible voice and an infectious stage presence that had the panelists dancing along as they sang “Vampire.” As Jenny McCarthy exclaimed during the performance, “Wow, she can sing!”

New panelist Rita Ora, who is filling in for Nicole Scherzinger this season, was also wowed by Goldfish’s performance. Toward the beginning of the song, she could be heard commenting, “Wow, what an amazing voice.”

The Season Premieres This Week

Season 11 of The Masked Singer is set to premiere on Wednesday, March 6, at 8/7c on FOX. This season will feature 16 masked celebrities competing to be named the winner, as the panelists try to guess their identities.

In addition to Goldfish, some of the other contestants this season include Book, Gumball, Lizard, Lovebird, Miss Cleocatra, Starfish, and Ugly Sweater. This season will also feature theme nights dedicated to everything from Queen to The Wizard of Oz.

If you’re wondering why longtime panelist Nicole Scherzinger sat this season out, it’s because the show’s filming schedule conflicted with her run as Norma Desmond in a production of Sunset Boulevard on London’s West End.
