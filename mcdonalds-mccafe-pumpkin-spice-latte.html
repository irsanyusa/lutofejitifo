McDonald's McCafe Pumpkin Spice Latte Is Back

Publish date: 2024-07-08

August 11, 2016 3:41 PM EDT

McDonald’s is officially bringing its McCafe Pumpkin Spice Latte back on Aug. 31, giving PSL enthusiasts the chance to satisfy their fall coffee cravings earlier than ever. The fast food giant will be offering the cinnamon-y sweet beverage in stores nationwide for the first time in three years starting later this month.

Introduced in 2013, the Mickey D’s latte is made “with a blend of freshly brewed 100 percent Rainforest Alliance Certified espresso and creamy steamed milk infused with a combination of warm spices and flavors of pumpkin pie,” Delish reports.

It will be available roughly one week prior to the day Starbucks’ version of the infamous seasonal coffee drink made its 2015 debut, Sept. 8.
