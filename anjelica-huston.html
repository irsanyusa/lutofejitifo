Profession: Actress

Biography: Anjelica Huston is an American actress who initially worked as a model in the 1970s and then had a breakthrough performance in Prizzi's Honor (1985).

She is the daughter of director John Huston and granddaughter of actor Walter Huston. Three generations of her family, her alongside both Walter and John, have won an Academy Award.
