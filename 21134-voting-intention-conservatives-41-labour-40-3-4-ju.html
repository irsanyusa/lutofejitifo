Voting Intention: Conservatives 41%, Labour 40% (3-4 July)

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Latest YouGov Westminster voting intention figures

The latest YouGov/Times voting intention survey sees the Conservatives on 41% (from 42% last week) and Labour on 40% (having been on 37% last week).

Elsewhere, Liberal Democrat voting intention stands at 9% (unchanged) while 11% would vote for other parties (from 12% last week).

When it comes to who would make the best Prime Minister, this week's poll shows 35% of people prefer Theresa May while 25% favour Jeremy Corbyn. A further 37% answered "not sure" and 2% refused to choose.
