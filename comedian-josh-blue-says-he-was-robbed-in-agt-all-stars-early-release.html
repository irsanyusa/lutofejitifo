America’s Got Talent: All-Stars has shared another early release performance from next week’s new episode, featuring Season 16 finalist Josh Blue. The comedian joked that he was robbed of a win, but now he’s back for another chance to be declared the best.

Josh Blue Performs in AGT: All-Stars Early Release

Blue came in third place during Season 16. When judge Howie Mandel asked him why he wanted to return for All-Stars, Blue replied, “I wanted to come back to the scene of the crime, because I was robbed.”

He went on to perform a comedy set in which he shared his funny thoughts on being a parent. As he joked, “Does anybody else think it’s weird that you can accidentally make a person? I can’t even on purpose make a bird house, but I’ve accidentally made two people.”

Blue also joked about how people reacted to him as a father with cerebral palsy. They would tell him, “Hey, didn’t anybody tell you not to shake a baby?” He would respond, “Well, I’m just holding him.”

What Did the Judges Think of Blue’s Performance?

All three judges gave Blue a standing ovation for his performance. As Heidi Klum told him, “What’s not to love about you?” Meanwhile, Simon Cowell predicted that the Superfans would vote Blue through to the Finals after that performance.

Howie Mandel brought up the phrase “laughter is the best medicine,” telling Blue, “I think you are the cure. You are the medicine. You gave us what we need more than anything else. It’s up to the Superfans now. You’re amazing. Thank you, Josh.”

Blue will be up against some tough competition this week, including past winners Kodi Lee and Brandon Leake. Find out if the Superfans chose him as an AGT: All-Stars finalist when the new episode airs Monday night at 8/7c on NBC.
