Milani Colour Statement Lipstick- Black Cherry

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Milani cosmetics have amazing make-up products at an incredibly affordable price. Their range of Colour Statement Lipsticks are intense and highly pigmented. The first Milani product that made me come to love the brand was the Milani Power Lip in Raspberry Tart (that was a good one!) if you missed the details on that, check here.

Milani Black Cherry is presented in a dominant gold-ish tube, a first look at the lipstick it contains reveals a nearly dark lush colour. The luxe and matured lipstick is a lightweight cream formula that contains Vitamins A, C and E that intensely moisturizes and nourishes the lips and its paraben free.

A stroke makes a full lip application that intensely moistens the lip, its rich and creamy- if you love your matte lipstick, this may be a bit 'runny' for you but otherwise its perfect, its also long lasting where if you go hours without touching up , you still have pale plum lips which are always effortlessly pretty!
