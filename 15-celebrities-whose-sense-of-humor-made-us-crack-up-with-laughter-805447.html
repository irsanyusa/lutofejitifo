15+ Celebrities Whose Sense of Humor Made Us Crack Up With Laughter

Publish date: 2024-06-19

It seems that the entire Internet is fond of Ryan Reynolds’ sense of humor. But this Hollywood actor is far from being the only show-business representative who uses his social media pages to make his followers laugh.

At Bright Side, we found 16 celebrities who have a great sense of humor. And in the bonus section, you’ll see another gem from the hilarious Ryan Reynolds.

Supermodel Heidi Klum wasn’t ashamed to post her old photos with an ironic comment.

Macaulay Culkin and his vision of possible sequels of Home Alone

And this funny online dialogue between Chris Evans and Jamie Lee Curtis happened due to fake news.

Last January, the media broke the news that Chris Evans was about to reprise the role of Captain America. This news shocked MCU fans as well as Evans himself (according to his tweet). Jamie Lee Curtis who played his mother in Knives Out decided to support him.

The actress who starred in the movie, The Parent Trap, couldn’t pass by the tweet that mentioned her and added fuel to the fire by quoting her character.

Emilia Clarke, who starred in Game of Thrones, also loves to give her photos funny captions.

The priceless moment when Courteney Cox recreated her Turkey dance from Friends

Courteney Cox, who played Monica on the Friends TV series, recreated her famous turkey dance on Thanksgiving Day. It was as funny as it was shown in the series. Even the sunglasses were there!

Chris Pratt let his fans know that a sense of humor runs in his family.

Actress Isla Fisher, who is married to Sacha Baron Cohen, also has a wonderful sense of humor.

Helen Mirren also knows how to make her followers smile.

And this is the dress.

Singer Shawn Mendes showed what can stand behind beautiful Instagram photos.

The singer posted his photo with Los Angeles in the background. He also attached a video showing his fall. The choice of the song for the video is quite suitable for the occasion — “Gravity” by John Mayer.

Julianne Moore is still looking for ways to appease the critics of her looks. Well, maybe this trick will work.

Actress Sarah Paulson created a poll on her Instagram to find out her followers’ opinions but then changed her mind quickly.

Comedian Melissa McCarthy knows how to make her followers laugh.

Her colleague, Rebel Wilson, is also good at it.

We love James Blunt, who is not afraid to be ironic about his reducing popularity.
